## ğŸ“¸ Character Portraits

Add a custom image to identify your characters:

### Adding a Portrait

1. When creating/editing a character
2. Tap the circular portrait area
3. Choose an image from your device
4. Image is cropped to a circle

### Tips for Portraits

- Use in-game screenshots of your character
- Crop to focus on the face
- Square images work best
- Images are stored locally

### Removing a Portrait

1. Edit the character
2. Tap the portrait
3. Select a blank/default image, or leave as-is
